Sindhudurg (rameshwaram-temple)

Rameshwaram temple is one of the four char dhama to attain moksha, holiest pilgrim place. Beautiful architecture, has 22 wells for holy bath, two Shiva linga (one of Mata sita, other brought by hanuman). The journey starts from bath in agni teertham outside and ends with dedicated pooja Arpan at mandir by pandiji charging Rs 3500 per person.
