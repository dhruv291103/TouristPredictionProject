Pune(Shinghad fort)

Earlier known as Kondhana, this historic fort perched atop Bhuleswar Range of Western Ghats houses old ramparts, monoliths and temple ruins dating back to the Maratha era.

Sangli(sangmeshwar-temple)

The Sangmeshwar Temple is one of the most sacred sites in Sangli. Lord Shiva is worshipped at this Sangmeshwar temple. A significant number of worshippers congregate here since this deity is highly regarded in Hindu mythology.

kolhapur(panhala-fort)

Panhala, a charming city in the Kolhapur district in the Indian state of Maharashtra, is a famous hill station. Located at an elevation of around 3200 feet above sea level, it is the lowest-lying incorporated municipality in the state. As a result, the Panhala Fort sees a lot of visitors.

Solapur(bhuikot-fort)

A must-see tourist destination whilst in the Solapur area is the Bhuikot Fort. During the Bahamani Dynasty's dominance in the 14th century AD, when it was constructed, the region was firmly rooted in the Middle Ages.
