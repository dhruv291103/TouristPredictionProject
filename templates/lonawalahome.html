Where2Go Lonawala What's Around Lonawala

Lonavala is one of the twin hill stations located near to each other, the other being Khandala.
These hill stations are very popular getaway spots for people from Mumbai and Pune,
which are very well-linked with Lonavala by road and rail.
Monsoons are the best time to visit Lonavala/Khandala.
