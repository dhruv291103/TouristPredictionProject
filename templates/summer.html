Climate & Season

Mid-June to Mid-September With the exception of the comparatively mild highlands in the southwest, summer signals the start of rising temperatures across most of the country, with the average hovering around 45 degrees C. The early mornings and long evenings are more temperate, so take advantage of the warm seas to explore Saudi’s world-class scuba diving sites, or try sailing and snorkeling. Don’t forget to pack sunscreen, lip balm, sunglasses, water shoes and a hat to protect your face.
