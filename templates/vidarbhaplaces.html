Where2Go Vidarbha What's Around Vidarbha

A coastal strip of land bounded by Sahyadri hills on the east and Arabian Sea on the west,
it is rich in mineral resources, and offers long stretches of clean,
sandy beaches and bustling seaside towns such as Ratnagiri and Ganapatipule.

chandrapur(bhadrawati-jain-temple)

The city's Bhadravati Jain Temple is a renowned place of worship for local Jain devotees. Elegant sculptures adorn the interior. The Central Railway will take you 32 kilometres to the settlement of Bhadravati, where you'll find the temple.

nagpur(Ramtek Temple)

It is widely held that Ram, Lakshman, and Sita slept at the holy city of Ramtek during their exile from Lanka during the Ramayana, making it a popular pilgrimage destination close to Nagpur. In addition, the Vakatakas ruled this area from the second to the fourth century, hence the city is full of interesting buildings and monuments from that time period.
