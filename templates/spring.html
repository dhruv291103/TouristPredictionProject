Climate & Season

Mid-March to Mid-June Spring in Saudi Arabia can be brisk, particularly in the north, where the temperature at night can drop below 15 degrees C. The central and southern regions are warmer, averaging around 20 degrees after nightfall. Rainfall is at its highest during spring, particularly in the central region and in the southwest over the Aseer Mountains. Don’t forget to pack layers — like light sweaters, scarves or pashminas — plus a rain jacket and sunglasses.
